EV Charging in the Phoenix Metro Area

Electric vehicle adoption continues to grow across the Phoenix metro area, supported by expanding public charging infrastructure and strong residential solar adoption. From Phoenix and Scottsdale to Tempe, Mesa, Chandler, and Gilbert, drivers are increasingly integrating EV charging into both daily commuting and longer regional travel.

The region’s extended summer temperatures and spread-out suburban layout make charging access and home installation planning particularly relevant for local EV owners. This site provides a clear, organized overview of public charging locations, charging types, and practical considerations for drivers throughout the Valley. Additional guidance on home installation, Tesla Superchargers, and desert climate factors will be added as this resource develops.

About This Resource

PhoenixEVCharging.com is an independent informational resource focused on EV charging infrastructure throughout the Phoenix metropolitan area. The site is designed to provide clear, organized insight into public charging availability, home installation considerations, and practical factors specific to driving and charging in a desert climate.

Content will continue to expand to include home EV charger installation planning, Tesla Supercharger coverage, and region-specific guidance relevant to drivers in the Valley.

  • Coverage: Phoenix, Scottsdale, Tempe, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, the West Valley, and surrounding communities.
  • Note:Charger availability, pricing, and site access details should always be confirmed through the original listing or network provider.

This site provides general informational content only and does not offer electrical contracting services or professional engineering advice. For installation, permitting, or code compliance guidance, consult a licensed electrician and the appropriate local jurisdiction.
